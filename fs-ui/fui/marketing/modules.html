Project Management Module

Complete project lifecycle management from initiation to completion

Key Capabilities

Project Creation & Tracking

Create projects with comprehensive details including budgets, timelines, teams, and service lines. Track project progress and status throughout the lifecycle.

Task Management

Break down projects into tasks and deliverables. Manage task assignments, dependencies, and progress tracking.

Task Board (Kanban)

Visual task management with drag-and-drop Kanban boards. Organize tasks by status and track workflow visually.

Task Planner (Gantt)

Timeline-based planning with Gantt charts. Visualize project schedules, dependencies, and milestones.

Resource Allocation

Plan and allocate resources efficiently. Track resource utilization and capacity across projects.

Project Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports including estimates vs actuals, completion reports, and performance analytics.

Benefits

  • Better resource utilization and planning

  • Data-driven project decisions
  • Streamlined project workflows

HRMS Module

Complete human resource management system

Key Capabilities

Employee Management

Comprehensive employee database with skills, certifications, designations, bands, grades, and organizational structure. Manage employee lifecycle from onboarding to exit.

Timesheet Management

Easy-to-use timesheet interface for logging work hours by project, task, and activity. Submit timesheets for approval with manager workflows.

Attendance Tracking

Track employee attendance, check-ins, and check-outs. Monitor attendance patterns and generate attendance reports.

Leave & Holiday Management

Manage employee leave requests, track leave balances, and configure company holidays. Streamline leave approval workflows.

Invoice Management

Generate and manage invoices for projects and clients. Track billing, payments, and financial transactions.

Organizational Structure

Manage organizational hierarchy with divisions, departments, sub-departments, teams, and designations. Flexible structure to match your organization.

Benefits

  • Centralized employee data management
  • Automated timesheet and attendance tracking
  • Streamlined HR processes
  • Better workforce planning
  • Compliance and reporting

Sales Module

Manage your sales pipeline and opportunities

Key Capabilities

Bid Request Management

Create and track bid requests from opportunity identification to conversion. Manage bid details, requirements, client information, and status throughout the sales process.

Purchase Order Tracking

Track purchase orders from creation to completion. Link purchase orders to projects, manage PO lifecycle, and track financial aspects.

Sales Pipeline

Visualize and manage your sales pipeline. Track opportunities, conversions, win rates, and sales performance metrics.

Client Management
