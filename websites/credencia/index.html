    Business Formation

    • Business Formation is the process by which an entity is created by law. It is not just a process of registering a business. A number of factors have to be considered for registering an entity. An entrepreneur must select a business structure considering its feasibility, merits, demerits, public acceptance & image of different types of business. We assist in the formation of the all types of entities including private limited companies, public limited companies, or a company to be registered under section 8 Company, wholly owned subsidiaries of Foreign Companies, joint venture companies, Limited Liability Partnership to name a few. We also help to the formation of charitable societies, trust, partnership firms and other type of entities.

    Compliance Management

    • The corporate compliance management primarily involves the strategies specially designed to detect and prevent violations of laws by the internal and external stakeholders for better control. Every Business entity is required to follow certain statutory compliances such as maintenance of various registers to filing of documents with regulators. Corporate Compliance is not merely a legal compliance but it is a necessity. A systematic well organised process of compliance helps in value building.
    • We provide all services with regard to regular compliances, event-based compliances and annual compliances. We also assist you to make policies, procedures, regulations and rules for better management the Company.

    Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)

    • Foreign direct investment (FDI) means an investment made by a company or individual in one country into business interests located in another country. Foreign direct investments can be made in a variety of ways, including the opening of a subsidiary or associate company in a foreign country, acquiring a controlling interest in an existing foreign company, or by means of a merger or joint venture with a foreign company. We help to facilitate the setting up of Branch / Project / Liaison offices under the Foreign Exchange Management Act and Corporate Laws in India, in case of foreign clients.

    Business Strategies

    • Business strategy is the firm's working plan for achieving its vision, prioritizing objectives, competing successfully, and optimizing financial performance with its business model.
    • There may be long term and short term plan of action designed to achieve a particular goal or set of goals or objectives of a Company. We provide expert services for making your business ideas into action.

    Corporate Restructuring

    • Restructuring is the process of reorganizing the legal, ownership, operational, or other structures of a company for the purpose of making it more profitable, or better organized for its present situation. We provide all services for financial and organisational restructuring viz; merger, amalgamation, take over, Business purchases, foreign collaborations, joint ventures, strategic alliance, etc.

    Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

    • Intellectual property rights (IPR) include patents, copyright, industrial design rights, trademarks, plant variety rights, trade dress, geographical indications etc. In the present corporate scenario Registration of intellectual property is extremely important to protect the ownership and performance of the Business. We provide assistance to registration of intellectual property rights.
